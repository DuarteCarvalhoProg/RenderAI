Capabilities

Built for creators

Whether you're a solo dev, a studio, or a student — SYNTH3D adapts to your workflow.

Core

Natural language → 3D geometry

Our model converts semantic descriptions into procedural 3D scene graphs, then renders them in Unity's real-time engine.

generate("ancient temple, overgrown with vines, moonlight")
Lighting

AI atmospheric lighting

Time of day, weather, and mood are all inferred from your prompt and applied automatically.

Iteration

Refine with follow-up prompts

Say "make it more foggy" or "add a river" and watch your scene update in real time.

Export

Export-ready assets

Download your scene as FBX, glTF, or a Unity package — ready to drop into your own project.
