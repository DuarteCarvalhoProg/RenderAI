Core Engine

Natural language to 3D

SYNTH3D's AI model understands spatial relationships, materials, lighting, atmosphere, and scale — all from a single sentence. No keywords, no special syntax. Just describe what you see in your mind.

  • ✦ Understands adjectives (foggy, ancient, brutalist, glowing)
  • ✦ Spatial awareness (behind, beneath, towering over)
  • ✦ Time & weather (dusk, midnight, rainy, blazing noon)
  • ✦ Art style inference (photorealistic, stylised, low-poly)
synth3d — prompt
$ generate
"A brutalist concrete library at dusk, long
shadows across cracked steps, warm light
from tall narrow windows, pigeons resting"

▸ 47 objects placed
▸ 12 material variants applied
▸ Directional light: 6500K, 18°
✔ Ready in 3.4s
Iteration

Refine with follow-up prompts

Your first prompt is never the last. Refine, adjust, and evolve your scene through conversation. SYNTH3D keeps context between prompts so every change builds on what came before.

  • ✦ "Make it more foggy and add some mist near the ground"
  • ✦ "Add a river running through the valley"
  • ✦ "Remove the trees on the left side"
  • ✦ "Change the time to just before sunrise"

Iterative by design

Export

Ready for any pipeline

Export your generated scene in industry-standard formats. Whether you're building a game, a cinematic, or an architectural visualisation — SYNTH3D outputs files that plug straight into your workflow.

  • ✦ .unitypackage — drop into any Unity project
  • ✦ .fbx / .obj — universal 3D formats
  • ✦ .glTF / .glb — for web & AR/VR
  • ✦ PNG / EXR — rendered stills at any resolution
